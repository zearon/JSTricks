Brief Introduction

JavaScript Tricks is a chrome extension that works as a user script library and allows you to create, edit, and manage all your user scripts. With this extension, your can make your user scripts automatically get executed when you open some websites, or even every website, or when you click on certain context menu items.

There are three item your can play with in this extension, The popup window, the context menu and the options page.
